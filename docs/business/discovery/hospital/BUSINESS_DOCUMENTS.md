## Patient Documents
- **Patient Record** — Demographics, contact, emergency contact, insurance
- **Medical Record** — Clinical history, diagnoses, treatments
- **Admission Form** — Inpatient registration and assessment
- **Discharge Summary** — Patient release documentation

## Clinical Documents
- **Prescription** — Medication order with dosage and frequency
- **Lab Report** — Test results and interpretation
- **Radiology Report** — Imaging findings and radiologist signature
- **Progress Notes** — Daily patient monitoring records

## Administrative Documents
- **Appointment Record** — Scheduled consultation details
- **Invoice** — Itemized billing for services
- **Insurance Claim** — Claim submission to insurer
- **Payment Receipt** — Payment confirmation
- **Employment Contract** — Staff agreement
- **Work Schedule** — Staff timetable

## Inventory Documents
- **Stock Receipt** — Inventory received from supplier
- **Stock Adjustment** — Inventory correction for expiry/damage
- **Requisition** — Internal stock request
