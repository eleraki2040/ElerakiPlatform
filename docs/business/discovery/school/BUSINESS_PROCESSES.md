# School Management - Business Processes

Version: 1.0
Status: In Progress
Last Updated: 2026-07-11

---

## 1. Student Registration & Admission Process
**Trigger:** Student applies for admission
**Participants:** Student, Parent, Receptionist, Administrator
**Flow:**
1. Application submitted online or in person
2. Documents verified (birth certificate, previous records)
3. Entrance assessment conducted (if applicable)
4. Admission decision made
5. Student record created in system
6. Class assigned based on grade level
7. Parent notified of admission status
**Ending Document:** Student Record, Admission Form

---

## 2. Course & Curriculum Management Process
**Trigger:** Academic year planning
**Participants:** Academic Head, Teachers, Administrator
**Flow:**
1. Curriculum framework reviewed
2. Courses defined for each grade
3. Teachers assigned to courses
4. Learning materials prepared
5. Schedule published
6. Students enrolled in courses
**Ending Document:** Course Catalog, Schedule

---

## 3. Class Scheduling Process
**Trigger:** Academic year start
**Participants:** Academic Head, Teachers, Students
**Flow:**
1. Time slots defined
2. Rooms assigned
3. Teacher availability checked
4. Student course conflicts resolved
5. Final schedule published
6. Schedules distributed to students and teachers
**Ending Document:** Class Schedule, Room Assignment

---

## 4. Attendance Tracking Process
**Trigger:** Daily class sessions
**Participants:** Teacher, Student, Parent
**Flow:**
1. Teacher marks attendance at start of class
2. System records presence/absence
3. Absences flagged automatically
4. Parents notified of unexplained absences
5. Attendance reports generated weekly/monthly
**Ending Document:** Attendance Record

---

## 5. Examination & Grading Process
**Trigger:** Assessment period
**Participants:** Teacher, Student, Administrator
**Flow:**
1. Exam schedule created
2. Exams conducted
3. Papers graded by teachers
4. Grades recorded in system
5. Academic head reviews final grades
6. Report cards generated
7. Report cards distributed to students and parents
**Ending Document:** Grade Report, Report Card

---

## 6. Fee Management Process
**Trigger:** Academic year or term
**Participants:** Accountant, Student, Parent
**Flow:**
1. Fee structure defined for academic year
2. Invoice generated for each student
3. Payment collected (cash, card, online)
4. Receipt issued
5. Outstanding balances tracked
6. Late payment reminders sent
**Ending Document:** Fee Invoice, Payment Receipt

---

## 7. Library Management Process
**Trigger:** Book request or return
**Participants:** Librarian, Student, Teacher
**Flow:**
1. Book catalog maintained
2. Borrowing recorded when book issued
3. Returns processed
4. Overdue books identified
5. Fines applied for overdue items
6. Damaged/lost books recorded
**Ending Document:** Borrowing Record, Fine Receipt

---

## 8. Transport Management Process
**Trigger:** Route assignment or daily transport
**Participants:** Transport Staff, Student, Parent
**Flow:**
1. Routes defined with stops and timings
2. Students assigned to appropriate routes
3. Daily transport tracked
4. Issues reported and resolved
5. Route changes communicated to parents
**Ending Document:** Route Assignment, Transport Log

---

## 9. Staff Management Process
**Trigger:** HR action
**Participants:** HR Manager, Staff, Administrator
**Flow:**
1. Recruitment and onboarding
2. Schedule created and published
3. Attendance tracked daily
4. Performance evaluated periodically
5. Payroll processed monthly
**Ending Document:** Employment Contract, Schedule, Payroll
