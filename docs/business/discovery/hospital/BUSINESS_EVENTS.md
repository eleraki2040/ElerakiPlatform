# Hospital Management - Business Events

Version: 1.0
Status: In Progress
Last Updated: 2026-07-11

---

## Patient Events
- **PatientRegistered:** New patient record created
- **PatientUpdated:** Patient information modified
- **PatientDeactivated:** Patient record deactivated

## Appointment Events
- **AppointmentScheduled:** New appointment created
- **AppointmentConfirmed:** Appointment confirmed by patient
- **AppointmentCancelled:** Appointment cancelled
- **AppointmentCompleted:** Consultation finished
- **AppointmentNoShow:** Patient did not attend

## Clinical Events
- **ConsultationStarted:** Doctor began examination
- **ConsultationCompleted:** Examination finished
- **PrescriptionIssued:** Medication prescribed
- **PrescriptionCancelled:** Prescription cancelled
- **LabOrderCreated:** Test ordered
- **LabResultRecorded:** Test result available
- **RadiologyOrderCreated:** Imaging ordered
- **RadiologyReportIssued:** Imaging result available

## Admission Events
- **AdmissionCreated:** Patient admitted
- **BedAssigned:** Bed allocated to patient
- **DischargeProcessed:** Patient discharged
- **TransferInitiated:** Patient moved between wards

## Pharmacy Events
- **MedicationDispensed:** Prescription filled
- **StockReceived:** Inventory received
- **StockAdjusted:** Inventory corrected
- **MedicationExpired:** Medication expired

## Billing Events
- **InvoiceGenerated:** Bill created
- **InsuranceClaimSubmitted:** Claim sent to insurer
- **InsuranceClaimApproved:** Claim approved
- **InsuranceClaimDenied:** Claim rejected
- **PaymentReceived:** Payment collected
- **InvoiceCancelled:** Bill voided

## Staff Events
- **StaffHired:** New employee joined
- **StaffUpdated:** Employee information changed
- **StaffTerminated:** Employee left
- **ScheduleCreated:** Work schedule published
- **AttendanceRecorded:** Staff attendance logged
