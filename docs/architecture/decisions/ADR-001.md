# ADR-001

Title:
Organization Engine Owns the Organizational Structure

Status:
Accepted

Date:
2026-07-05

Owner:
Architecture Team

---

# Context

The Eleraki Platform is designed as a modular business platform that supports multiple industries such as:

- ERP
- CRM
- Hospital
- School
- Factory
- Government
- NGO

Every business application requires a common organizational structure.

Without a dedicated owner for organizational data, multiple engines would duplicate or modify the same information, leading to inconsistencies and tight coupling.

The platform therefore requires a single authoritative source for organizational information.

---

# Decision

The Organization Engine is the exclusive owner of:

- Organization
- Organization Unit
- Organization Unit Type

No other Engine may create, modify, or delete these entities directly.

Other Engines may only reference organizational data through published interfaces.

---

# Rationale

This decision provides:

- Single Source of Truth
- Clear ownership boundaries
- High cohesion
- Low coupling
- Better maintainability
- Easier future scalability

It also aligns with the Domain-Driven Design principle that every business concept has exactly one owning Bounded Context.

---

# Consequences

Positive

- Eliminates duplicated organizational data.
- Prevents conflicting business logic.
- Simplifies maintenance.
- Improves consistency across the platform.
- Enables independent engine evolution.

Negative

- Other Engines must depend on Organization Engine contracts.
- Changes to organizational data must always pass through Organization Engine.

These trade-offs are acceptable because organizational consistency has higher priority than implementation convenience.

---

# Alternatives Considered

## Alternative 1

Allow every Engine to maintain its own organizational structure.

Decision

Rejected.

Reason

This leads to duplicated data, synchronization problems, and inconsistent business rules.

---

## Alternative 2

Store organizational information inside Identity Engine.

Decision

Rejected.

Reason

Identity and organizational structure represent different business domains with different responsibilities.

---

## Alternative 3

Use a shared database table owned by multiple Engines.

Decision

Rejected.

Reason

Shared ownership violates bounded contexts and creates tight coupling.
