# ADR-003

Title:
Budget Allocation Strategy

Status:
Accepted

Date:
2026-07-05

Owner:
Architecture Team

---

## CONTEXT

The Organization Domain requires a mechanism for allocating budgets to organization units. This decision affects financial planning, reporting, and operational control.

## DECISION

We will use a **Manual Allocation** approach where organization managers allocate budgets to their units.

## RATIONALE

1. **Flexibility:** Manual allocation allows for business context and strategic priorities
2. **Control:** Managers have the best understanding of their unit's needs
3. **Simplicity:** No complex formulas required initially
4. **Auditability:** Manual decisions are easier to trace and justify

## CONSEQUENCES

### Positive
- Direct control over resource allocation
- Ability to respond to strategic priorities
- Simple implementation

### Negative
- Requires active management
- Potential for inconsistent allocation across organizations
- May not scale well for large organizations

## MIGRATION PATH

Future versions may introduce:
- Percentage-based allocation
- Formula-based allocation
- Automated suggestions
